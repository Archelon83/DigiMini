Dusk, God of Lies and Deception

Appearance

Dusk's appearance is everchanging, He is a shapeshifter that has mastered blending into anywhere. When he is revealed in his true form he is a tall slender man with a face that is covered by a stark white mask with no mouth. No one has seen what is under that mask.

Personality & Temperment

Cold, calculating, and deceitful. No one, not even his own cult know his true motives. He is a mysterious enigma but one thing is clear... his intentions are not pure.

Potential Plot Hooks

  • Dusk has heard of the parties meddling in the affairs of the gods. An assassin in a white mask attacks the party one night while they are resting. If they manage to fight off this assassin, they find the only thing on them is the dark robes they are dressed in, a slender knife the glints in the moonlight, and a black envelope with a broken white mask seal. The letter in the envelope simply lists the party members by name. If they try to remove the assassin's mask, they find that his face is completely featureless.
  • A local gang of thugs has been terrorizing the town that the party has come across. They have been requiring the local shopkeepers and townspeople to pay them a fine for "protection", and if they do not comply they are found beaten within an inch of their life. Rumor has it that they have a hidden storehouse that they work out of here in town, but nobody knows where it is. The thugs seem to appear and disappear just as quickly from their target destination. Maybe the players can investigate and discover this hideout.
